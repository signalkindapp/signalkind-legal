Privacy Policy for SignalKind

Effective date: 9 April 2026

SignalKind (“SignalKind”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the SignalKind app.

1. About SignalKind

SignalKind is a self-awareness and lifestyle tracking app designed to help users record, reflect on, and better understand patterns in mood, behaviour, routines, and wellbeing.

2. Information You Enter Into the App

SignalKind may allow you to enter and store information such as:
	•	diary or journal entries
	•	mood and emotional tracking data
	•	behaviour and lifestyle tracking data
	•	medication tracking information
	•	app settings and preferences
	•	other content you choose to add within the app

This information is stored locally on your device and, if you enable iCloud sync, is synchronised through Apple iCloud using CloudKit.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

SignalKind does not store your personal entries, diary content, mood logs, medication records, or other user-created wellbeing data on developer-owned servers.

Your data is stored:
	•	locally on your device; and
	•	if iCloud sync is enabled, in your own Apple iCloud account through Apple’s CloudKit services

This means your data is handled through Apple’s infrastructure rather than our own servers.

4. Subscriptions

SignalKind offers optional paid subscriptions. Subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store.

We do not receive your full payment card or banking details. We may have access only to limited information necessary to confirm subscription status and provide access to paid features.

5. How Information Is Used

Information handled by SignalKind is used only to:
	•	provide the app’s features
	•	save and display your entries and tracked information
	•	sync your data across your Apple devices if iCloud sync is enabled
	•	manage subscription access
	•	maintain app functionality and reliability
	•	respond to support enquiries if you contact us

We do not sell your personal data.

We do not use your entries or personal wellbeing information for advertising.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We do not share your diary entries, mood data, medication logs, or other user-created content with advertisers or data brokers.

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is transmitted through Apple iCloud / CloudKit so that it can sync across your Apple devices.

7. Third-Party Services

SignalKind relies on Apple services only where necessary to operate key functionality, including:
	•	Apple App Store for subscriptions and payments
	•	Apple iCloud / CloudKit for optional data synchronisation

SignalKind does not use third-party advertising networks or external cloud databases to store user-entered wellbeing content.

8. Data Retention

Your information remains on your device and, where enabled, within your iCloud account unless you delete it.

If you remove content from the app or delete the app, data may remain in iCloud unless you also remove it from your iCloud account or disable iCloud sync in accordance with Apple’s services and settings.

9. Security

We take reasonable steps to design SignalKind in a way that respects and protects user privacy.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Choices

You can control your information by:
	•	choosing what information to enter into the app
	•	deleting entries within the app
	•	enabling or disabling iCloud sync through your device and app settings
	•	managing subscriptions through your Apple App Store account settings

11. Children’s Privacy

SignalKind is not intended for children under 13, or any higher minimum age required by local law. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

12. Medical Disclaimer

SignalKind is a self-awareness and lifestyle tracking tool only.

It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment, and it is not a substitute for professional healthcare, mental health support, or therapy.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we do, we will update the effective date shown above.
